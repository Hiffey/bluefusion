MAZAGINE DESIGN

A periodic publication containing pictures and stories and articles of interest to those who purchase it or subscribe to it.

Detailed Information

Magazine paginates with each issue starting at page three, with the standard sizing being 8.16 x 10.98 inches. However, in the technical sense, a journal has continous pagination throughout a volume. Mazagines can be distributed through the mail, through sales by newsstands, bookstores or other vendors, or through free distribution at selected pick-up locations.
